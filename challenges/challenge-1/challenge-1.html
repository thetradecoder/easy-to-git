Challenge #1

Description: Now that you've added your name to the contributors.md file, its time to change some actual code. The following steps will have you add your own camper cell, html elements, and custom styling.

Steps:

  1. In the cellPanel div, add your own div and give it a class of 'camperCell'
  2. Give your div a unique id
  3. Inside your div, add an h1 element that has your name
  4. Inside your div, add an image that has a picture/avatar of you
  5. Inside your div, add a p element that contains your favorite quote
  6. Add any custom styling by targeting your custom id in the campers.css file
